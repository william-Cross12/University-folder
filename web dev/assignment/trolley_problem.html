About The Trolley Problem

You will be given multiple scenarios and will need to decide if you would pull the lever to move the train to the other track or not

(if you do pull the lever does that make you responsible for their deaths?)

Trolley Scenario One

- On the top track there are 3 stranger,
- On the bottom track there is 1 stranger.

Trolley Scenario Two

- On the top track there is one stranger,
- On the bottom track there are 3 strangers.

Trolley Scenario Three

- On the top track is a person of 66 who is 3 days from retirement,
- On the bottom track there is a child of 4 with a loving family.

Trolley Scenario Four

- On the top track there is a child of 4 with a loving family,
- On the bottom track is a person of 66 who is 3 days from retirement.
